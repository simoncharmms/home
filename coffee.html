The Bean

What's in your cup — organic espresso from Supremo, Unterhaching (near Munich).

Supremo Bio Espresso

Organic certified espresso blend from one of Munich's finest specialty roasters, founded in 2005. Direct trade sourced with focus on sustainability and quality.

Organic / Bio Direct Trade Specialty

Supremo Coffee Roasters

Family-run since 2005 in Unterhaching. Known as one of Germany's best roasteries (Der Feinschmecker 2025). Board members of the Alliance for Coffee Excellence and Cup of Excellence.

Est. 2005 Unterhaching
